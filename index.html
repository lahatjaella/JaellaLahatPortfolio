Eco Apparel

Above is a slideshow introducing a project I am currently spearheading the development of, entitled Eco Apparel. The Eco Apparel web application aims to promote sustainability in the fashion industry. This project involves utilizing data analysis techniques and a range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and APIs. My role includes driving the iterative development of essential features such as a brand sustainability assessment, a brand alternative Hall of Fame, user accounts, quizzes, and donation pages.
